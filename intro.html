Welcome to ARKHumanity Triage page.

This interface will enable an individual to label and classify at-risk tweets as they are streamed into the application after being filtered. This labeling process helps train our dataset and in turn to improve our machine learning algorithm to better identify people on social media who are in crisis or experiencing acute risk of suicidality. This application collects public messages from Twitter that include phrases associated with suicidal thinking so that they be reviewed. During review, the messages may be labeled according the risk of suicidal behavior. There are 5 different labels in order of increasing intensity of risk.

None

Please label the level of risk as “None” if there is no indication of risk of suicidal behavior. This may include messages that make no indication of distress or reference to suicidal thinking. It may also include messages that discuss suicide in some way but do not indicate that the author is distressed such as campaigns for mental health awareness, reporting on celebrity suicide, and flippant references to suicide.

Low

Please label the level of risk as “Low” if the person is distressed but there are no observed risk factors for suicidal behavior.

Moderate

Please label the level of risk as “Moderate” if the person is distressed and there are observed risk factors for suicidal behavior, but there is no evidence of suicidal ideation.

High

Please label the level of risk as “High” if the person reveals suicidal ideation, but there is not evidence of intent.

Immediate

Please label the level of risk as “Immediate” if the person reveals suicidal thinking and there is evidence of immediate risk. This may include describing a plan, a method, preparation, or a history of suicidal behavior.
